Welcome to HuangShiGang University.

HuangShiGang University is located in Huangshi City, Hubei Province, beside Cihu Lake, covering an area of more than 600000 square meters.
It is a key comprehensive normal university directly under the Ministry of education and a key construction University of the "211 Project". It is an important base for the state to cultivate teachers and other senior professionals in secondary and higher education institutions.
There are 31 teaching and research units, and 75 undergraduate majors are enrolled nationwide, ranging from undergraduates to masters and doctoral students. There are currently more than 31,000 full-time students of various types, including more than 18,000 undergraduates, more than 11,000 graduate students, and more than 3,800 teachers.
At present, all teachers and students are working hard with high morale to build the school into a research-oriented high-level university with distinctive teacher education characteristics!
